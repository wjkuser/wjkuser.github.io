About

Undergraduate researcher working toward general-purpose generative models.

I am currently pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science at Beihang University. I am interested in efficient, controllable, and multimodal generative models, and I am especially excited about the possibility of unifying video generation and language generation within a broader generative intelligence framework.

Research Interests

Efficient and unified generative intelligence.

Video Generation

Fast and high-quality video generation, including attention efficiency, temporal structure, and controllability.

Multimodal Generation

Models that connect language, vision, video, and embodied interaction through shared representations.

Unified Generative Models

Bridging video generation and text generation toward more general and coherent generative systems.
